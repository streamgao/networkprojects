Mindful Lamp Privacy Policy



This privacy policy covers Lift Labs' collection and treatment of information and data collected when you visit Mindful Lamp's Websites or use our Products or Services.

HOW MINDFUL LAMP COLLECTS DATA?

1. Public Feeds in Social Media Accounts. Mindful Lamp collects data form your social media accounts. We plan to include your Facebook, Twitter, Instagram accounts, and/or others. Get your daily public feeds in these accounts then analyze. We have already included Facebook account, others will be provided soon.
2. Information you provide. If you want, you could tell us how you are feeling today. And this takes 80% of your total emotion value. It is coming soon.
3. Other methods. Tone analysis. Typing habit emotion analysis. etc. All for future consideration.

HOW IS DATA USED BY MINDFUL LAMP?

1. The data referenced in this Privacy Policy is primarily used by Mindful Lamp to calculate your emotion value, so our lamp can change its attributes by these.  And then help you adjust, i.e. meditate, release stress.
2. Email addresses and any other information supplied by You may also be used by us to respond to emails, phone calls and requests. In addition we may use the data that identifies the Product IDs associated with Your account to provide information and technical assistance to You.

HOW IS DATA KEPT SECURE?

1. Data collected by Mindful Lamp via its Websites and Services is stored on secure third-party servers.
